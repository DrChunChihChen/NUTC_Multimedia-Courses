About me

Hello, my name is Elvis (陳俊智) and I am honored to introduce myself as a researcher and Assistant Professor in the field of machine learning, circular economy, energy economy, and sustainability at the National Taichung University of Science and Technology in Taiwan. My passion for this field is evident in my numerous publications in reputable journals. In 2020, I earned a PhD from the Management Science Department at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. During my studies, I had the opportunity to gain advanced knowledge and skills as a visiting scholar at North Carolina State University, where I specialized in data analysis, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and heuristic algorithms. This experience enabled me to deepen my understanding of these subjects and apply my knowledge to make meaningful contributions in my field. One notable achievement was the development of the Artificial Intelligent Manufacturing Parameter Process System (AIMPPS) for a car steering wheel manufacturing company, which was subsequently published in the International Journal of Advanced Manufacturing Technology in 2022. I am a highly analytical and resourceful problem-solver who is able to extract valuable insights from data to inform decision-making and problem-solving. This makes me an adept collaborator and a valuable asset to any project. My recently funded project, which aims to simulate the dynamic relationship between the EU's circular economy, energy consumption, environmental sustainability, and economic growth using deep learning methods, received funding from the National Science and Technology Council. I am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow and welcome collaborations with colleagues from diverse backgrounds and perspectives, as this enhances my ability to work as part of a team and achieve common goals. In addition to my professional achievements, I bring a good sense of humor and a passion for sports to any project. I am open to discussing potential collaborations or any other relevant topics and look forward to connecting with you.

Projects

國科會(National Science and Technology Council)-以深度學習方法模擬歐盟循環經濟、能源消耗、環境永續、與經濟成長之系統動態模型(Simulating the dynamic relationship between the EU's circular economy, energy consumption, environmental sustainability, and economic growth using deep learning methods) -

The project uses the System dynamics method to frame European’s circular economy, energy consumption, carbon emissions, and economic factors model from 2000 to 2020. And by using deep learning methods to predict each factor to 2030. The system dynamics model of the "circular economy-energy consumptioncarbon emissions-economic growth" is constructed to simulate the linkage of important factors such as circular economy indicators (1. Production and consumption、2. Waste management, 3. Secondary raw materials, 4. Competitiveness and innovation), real GDP, carbon emissions, consumption of different energy (clean/non-clean). Using the European circular economy experience offers the Taiwan government a suitable policy reference to achieve sustainable development goals
