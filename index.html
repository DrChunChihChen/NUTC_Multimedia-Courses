Courses & Workshops

Python Programming and Application

The course "Python Programming and Application" covers the basics of programming using the Python language, as well as how to apply these skills in real-world applications.

Internet Multimedia Application

An internet multimedia application course teaches students how to create, design, and process multimedia content for various purposes. It covers topics such as sound, images, interactivity, and multimedia technology.

Research Method

A research methods course teaches the principles and practices of conducting research, including research design, data collection and analysis, and research ethics. It may also introduce different research methodologies and provide practical experience in conducting research. The goal is to equip students with the skills to design and conduct their own research projects.
